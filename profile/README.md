## Our Mission

At Sculptor AI, we believe AI shouldn't be locked away in corporate towers. We are democratizing AI by building powerful, open-source tools that anyone can use, modify, and improve. Our goal is to empower students and developers to explore AI technology and push the boundaries of what's possible.

## Featured Projects

### Ursa Minor AI Model

Our flagship AI model is designed to enhance various AI applications. It offers advanced reasoning capabilities combined with lightning-fast performance. We have ensured it remains fully open source and accessible while targeting state-of-the-art accuracy.

[![Try Ursa](https://img.shields.io/badge/Try_Ursa_Now-Launch-2ea44f?style=for-the-badge)](https://ai.sculptorai.org/)
[![Hugging Face](https://img.shields.io/badge/Hugging_Face-FFD21E?style=for-the-badge&logo=huggingface&logoColor=black)](https://huggingface.co/Sculptor-AI)

### Constellation Platform

This open-source AI platform is built to democratize artificial intelligence. It features local model support with complete privacy, OpenRouter integration for unified API access, and an advanced media studio for AI-powered processing with real-time capabilities.

[![Explore Constellation](https://img.shields.io/badge/Explore_Constellation-Launch-0366d6?style=for-the-badge)](https://ai.sculptorai.org/)
